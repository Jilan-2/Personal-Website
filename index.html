About Me

Hello! I'm Jilan, a versatile Electrical Engineering undergraduate with a passion for innovation and technology. My journey began with a foundation in web development, which sparked my interest in the intersection of electrical engineering and programming.

Over time, I've honed my skills through self-directed learning and hands-on experience, tackling mechanical and electronic challenges with enthusiasm. While it's been a while since I've pursued web development as a project, I'm eager to reignite my passion and delve deeper into the world of coding.

Currently, I'm setting my sights on becoming an integral part of the electrical and programming division at Nala13 within Barunastra, a pioneering research team at Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology which focuses on developing unmanned vessels to drive advancements in maritime technology worldwide.

I thrive in collaborative environments, where I can leverage my expertise and learn from others to tackle complex challenges. As I continue to grow and evolve, I'm excited to contribute my skills and passion to projects that push the boundaries of innovation.
